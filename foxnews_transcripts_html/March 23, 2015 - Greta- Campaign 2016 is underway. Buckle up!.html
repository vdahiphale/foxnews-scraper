it's official: Campaign 2016 is underway. And I have no idea what twists and turns will develop. But it's worth taking a second and thinking about Campaign 2016 and about coverage and anticipated candidates, starting with Governor Jeb Bush and Secretary Hillary Clinton. Both have family members who have held high office, in fact, the highest. But both Bush and Clinton should be judged on their individual records, whether it be accomplishments and failings. Their family members are not running; they are.
Likewise, at some point, if former Maryland governor Martin O'Malley announces, it is fair to give my comments extra scrutiny because my husband is a long-time friend of Governor O'Malley.
