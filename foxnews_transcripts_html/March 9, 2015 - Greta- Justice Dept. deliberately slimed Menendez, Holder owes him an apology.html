Let's go "Off the Record" for a minute. Late Friday, the Justice Department leaked that later this month, not now, but at some future unspecified date, the Justice Department will bring corruption charges against Democratic senator, Bob Menendez. Now, I have no idea if the senator did anything wrong or not, but I do know this: The manner in which this was leaked is a deliberate sliming of the US senator.
It would be one thing if the news were unsealing of an indictment based on testimony, but it is not. This is just an ugly, anonymous smear from the Eric Holder Justice Department, deliberately leaked to the media late on a Friday night. And I think the leak was intentionally done then so the Sunday morning talk shows would pick it up and talk about Senator Menendez, as though is he some kind of ax murderer and the crook.
