Let's go "Off the Record" for just a minute. Now, this is not about whether you are for or against ObamaCare. This is about reality, about getting the money to fund ObamaCare. Where are we getting it? ObamaCare for the nation is not free. We had to find the money but where? Here is who the Obama administration is banking on, the young and the healthy.
President Obama believes the young and healthy will finance the old and not-so-healthy. Young people typically don't need much medical care since they have less wear and tear on their bodies. They are young. So, the hope is that the young fill the ObamaCare covers and take that money to use to pay the big medical bills of the older and the sick. Can we count on the young? Will they do this for us? What do you think?

Well, the White House is hoping the young will make up 40 percent of the new enrollees in the health care exchanges. But getting that many people to sign up, well, I don't think that's so easy. Right now, the young people are laughing at ObamaCare. Yes, laughing -- that doesn't help. Laughing at the amateur dotcom website the federal government put together, a website many of them may think they could create themselves and actually have it work.
