Let's all go "Off the Record" for just a minute. The Obama administration is reportedly negotiating a prisoner swap with the Taliban to release a captured American soldier. It's tough to think of negotiating with the Taliban. They are evil, but I'm all for getting our POW home and as soon as possible.
But while we are on the topic of the Taliban, let's talk about the elephant in the room. The United States has repeatedly promised the women of Afghanistan that we will not abandon them to the Taliban and, we're doing that we're leaving. And the Taliban is moving right back in. No doubt about it, we are abandoning the women.
