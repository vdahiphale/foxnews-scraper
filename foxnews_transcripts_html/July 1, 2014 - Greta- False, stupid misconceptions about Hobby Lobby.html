Let's all go "Off the Record" for a minute. I don't know, but when I read some stuff online, I just want to pull my hair out. Some things are so stupid, so sloppy, and sometimes deliberately skewed to advance some selfish political agenda.
Today's example? Hobby Lobby. Many saying that Hobby Lobby is opposed to contraception for their employees. That's not only false, it's stupid. Sometimes I wonder if people actually take dumb pills because even a dimwit doing a tad bit of research would know Hobby Lobby does pay for some, but not all, contraception. It pays for the pill, the diaphragm, the patch, the ring, and other forms of contraception. What Hobby Lobby doesn't pay for are IUDs and the so-called "Morning After" or "Week After" pill, because those types violate their religious beliefs.
