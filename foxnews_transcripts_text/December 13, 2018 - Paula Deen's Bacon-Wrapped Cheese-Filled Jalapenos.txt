Place jalapeno peppers in an air fryer basket. Set temperature to 400 degrees. Cook for 4 minutes. Let peppers sit in air fryer for 5 minutes. Remove peppers from basket, peel off loosened skins, then slice each pepper in half lengthwise. With a sharp knife, remove membranes and seeds and rinse well. Pat dry with paper towels.
